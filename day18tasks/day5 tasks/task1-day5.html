Save the Tiger's

Description :Save the Tigers is a Telugu-language Indian comedy-drama television series about three frustrated husbands who rant about their marital problems, which go viral and lead to a series of strange events. The series has 13 episodes over two seasons, and first aired on April 27, 2023. It is available to stream on Netflix
